Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026 · Last updated: May 2, 2026

Shepherd AI ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website bs.thomasperdana.com (the "Service"). This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. What data we collect

When you use Shepherd AI, we may collect the following information:

  • Account data — when you sign up via email, Google, or GitHub, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from our authentication provider (Clerk). We do not see or store your password.
  • Study data — Bible passages you study, the education level you select, the generated study content, and recommendations you accept are saved to your account so you can revisit them later.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, and browser type are collected automatically through standard server logs and may be used for troubleshooting.

2. How we use your data

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To generate Bible studies tailored to your selected education level.
  • To save your study history so you can revisit past studies.
  • To provide personalized study recommendations.
  • To improve the Service and debug issues.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

3. Third-party services we use

Shepherd AI relies on the following third-party providers, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Clerk — authentication and user management. Clerk Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — database hosting for study history, profiles, and journals. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • DeepSeek (via OpenRouter) — AI study generation and recommendations. OpenRouter Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — hosting and deployment. Vercel Privacy Policy

When you generate a study, the passage reference and education level are sent to the DeepSeek API to produce content. No personal identifiers are included in those requests.

4. Cookies

We use essential session cookies managed by Clerk to keep you signed in. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies.

5. Data retention

Your study history and profile are retained for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account (via the Clerk user portal or by contacting us), all associated data is removed from our database within 30 days.

6. Your rights

  • Access — you can view your profile and study history anytime from the app dashboard.
  • Delete — you can delete individual studies from your history, clear your entire history, or request full account deletion by contacting us.
  • Export — you can export any study as a PDF directly from the app.
  • Correction — you can update your profile information through the Clerk user portal.

7. Children's privacy

Shepherd AI is a Bible study tool designed for all ages. The "5th Grade" study level is written for young readers, but the Service is not directed at children under 13 as an unsupervised audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
