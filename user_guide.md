# Shepherd AI — User Guide

**Shepherd AI** is a comprehensive KJV Bible study platform built on Reformed theology. Generate studies at four education levels, track your spiritual growth, join study groups, and explore Scripture through cross-reference tools.

**URL:** https://bs.thomasperdana.com

---

## Getting Started

### Sign Up / Sign In
1. Go to https://bs.thomasperdana.com
2. Click **Sign In** (top right)
3. Sign up with Google, GitHub, or Facebook
4. Your profile is auto-created on first sign-in

### The Dashboard
After signing in, you land on `/app` — the study dashboard with:
- **Passage input** — type any Bible reference (e.g., `John 3:16`, `Romans 8:28-30`, `Psalm 23`)
- **Level selector** — choose your study depth
- **Quick links** to Assessment, Journal, Curriculum, and more

---

## Core Features

### 1. Bible Study Generator

Generate in-depth Bible studies at four education levels:

| Level | Audience | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 🟢 Level 1 | 5th Grade (age 10-11) | Simple, concrete, memorable — big idea, memory verse, activity |
| 🟡 Level 2 | High School (age 14-18) | Real, relevant, honest — hook, text, clash, this week |
| 🔵 Level 3 | College (undergrad) | Critical thinking — thesis, word study, interpretive options, Reformed view |
| 🔴 Level 4 | PhD (seminary) | Exegesis, scholarship — parsing, scholarly debate, original contribution |

**How to use:**
1. Type a passage in the search box (e.g., `Romans 8:28`)
2. Click a level button (🟢🟡🔵🔴)
3. Wait for AI generation (3-15 seconds)
4. Read the study — each section is color-coded by level
5. Click **📄 PDF** to download a formatted PDF
6. Click **📤 Share** to share with the community

**Theological Focus Selector:**
Below the level buttons, choose your theological lens:
- ✝️ **Christological** — traces what Jesus said and did
- 🔺 **Trinitarian** — traces Father, Son, and Spirit together
- 🏛️ **Theological** — traces 7 systematic theology loci

Every study includes **Hope · Faith · Love** (1 Corinthians 13:13) — what to believe, hope for, and how to love.

### 2. Thompson Chain Reference 🔗

Trace a topic through the entire Bible, verse by verse.

**How to use:**
After generating a study, scroll down to the **Chain Reference** panel:
1. **📖 Related tab** — see which topical chains contain your passage
2. Click any chain to expand it — view all verses in order
3. See your passage highlighted with its position (e.g., "verse 6 of 15")
4. Use **🗂️ Browse** to search all 50 chains by keyword
5. Use **🔍 Search** to find chains by topic (e.g., "faith", "prayer", "love")
6. Click **🤖 AI-Generate** for a custom chain on any passage

**50 curated topics:** God's Love, Holiness, Faithfulness, Sovereignty, Trinity, Messianic Prophecies, Names of Christ, The Cross, Resurrection, Second Coming, Holy Spirit, Fruit of the Spirit, Spiritual Gifts, Salvation, Grace, Faith, Repentance, Justification, Adoption, Assurance, Eternal Life, Scripture, Sin, Temptation, Forgiveness, Sanctification, Prayer, Love, Hope, Joy, Peace, Humility, Obedience, Suffering, Comfort, Trials, The Church, Worship, Fellowship, Lord's Supper, Baptism, Heaven, Judgment, Resurrection of the Dead, New Covenant, Promises, Discipleship, Great Commission, Abiding in Christ, Fear of the Lord, Marriage, Parenting, Faith-Hope-Love Triad

### 3. Intertestament Cross-References 📜

See how the Old and New Testaments speak to each other about any passage.

**How to use:**
After generating a study, scroll to the **What the Testaments Say** panel:
1. View OT foundations and NT fulfillments in collapsible panels
2. Each reference shows full KJV verse text and a connection type badge:
   - 🗣️ **Direct Quote** — NT explicitly quotes OT
   - ✅ **Fulfillment** — prophecy fulfilled
   - 🔗 **Typology** — OT foreshadows NT reality
   - 💭 **Allusion** — NT echoes OT theme
   - 📖 **Thematic** — doctrinal parallel
3. Click **🤖 AI: How Christ Fulfills [passage]** for AI-generated analysis with Gospel citations

**24 curated connections:** Creation, Protoevangelium, Abrahamic Covenant, Passover Lamb, The Law, Davidic Covenant, Psalm 22 (Crucifixion), Psalm 110 (Priestly King), Virgin Birth, Suffering Servant, New Covenant, Christ + Law, Word Became Flesh, Serpent Lifted Up, Justification, Adam/Christ, Golden Chain, Hall of Faith, Melchizedek, New Creation, Faith-Hope-Love, The Shepherd, The Temple

### 4. Assessment 🧠

21-question diagnostic to determine your Bible knowledge level.

**Path:** Navigation → **Assess**
1. Answer 21 questions across 7 dimensions
2. View your results with a radar chart
3. Your recommended study level is auto-suggested
4. Re-assess anytime to track growth

---

### 5. Journal & Fruit Tracker 📓

Private journaling with Fruit of the Spirit tracking.

**Path:** Navigation → **Journal**
1. Create entries with mood, gratitude, and passage linking
2. Rate yourself on the Fruit of the Spirit (Galatians 5:22-23 + James 3:17-18)
3. Toggle **privacy locks** — entries marked "Struggle" or "Prayer Request" never leave private context
4. Track growth over time with the fruit score radar

---

### 6. Curriculum Paths 🗺️

Structured Bible study curriculum for all levels.

**Path:** Navigation → **Curriculum**
- 🟢 **Seeker's Path** — 12 modules · Foundation
- 🟡 **Disciple's Path** — 16 modules · Growth
- 🔵 **Teacher's Path** — 24 modules · Depth
- 🔴 **Scholar's Path** — 12 modules (ongoing) · Mastery

Each module includes: passage, themes, objectives, key questions, memory verse, Christological connection.

Click **Study This Passage** to jump to the study generator, or **Generate Workbook** for a 4-session study workbook.

---

### 7. Workbook Generator 📚

AI-generated 4-session study workbooks with PDF export.

**Path:** Curriculum → any module → **Generate Workbook**
- 4 sessions per workbook (Opening Prayer, Read, Observe, Interpret, Apply, Dig Deeper, Memory Verse, Closing Prayer)
- Export to PDF with one click

---

### 8. Flywheel Analytics 📊

30-day spiritual growth report with 7-dimension scorecard.

**Path:** Navigation → **Flywheel**
- Circular score gauge (overall growth score)
- 7-dimension radar chart: study consistency, journal depth, prayer life, scripture memory, application, community engagement, growth trajectory
- GitHub-style 30-day activity heatmap
- Plateau detection with AI-generated suggestions
- Personalized narrative report

---

### 9. Community & Sharing 👥

Share study insights and encourage others.

**Path:** Navigation → **Community**
- **Feed tab** — shared study insights with passage links and level badges
- **Testimonies tab** — anonymous testimonies (Pray/Encourage)
- **Prayer Wall tab** — opt-in prayer requests
- **Share tab** — share a study insight or journal reflection

**Privacy Enforcement:** Prayer, struggle, and prayer_request fields are stripped at the API level before any content is shared.

---

### 10. Study Groups 🏠

Create or join study groups with invite codes.

**Path:** Navigation → **Groups**
1. **Create a group** — name, description, auto-generated 6-character invite code
2. **Join a group** — enter an invite code
3. View members and their roles (leader/teacher/member)
4. **Generate Leader's Guide** — AI creates a session plan with discussion questions, leader notes, tough Q&A, group size adaptations, and homework

---

### 11. Study History 📋

Your past studies are saved automatically.

- Last 20 studies saved to Supabase + browser localStorage
- Click any past study to reload it
- Clear history anytime

---

### 12. Recommendations 💡

After each study, click **"What should I study next?"** for 3-tier AI recommendations:
- 🌱 **Beginner** — next foundational passage
- 🌿 **Intermediate** — deeper exploration
- 🌳 **Expert** — advanced theological study

Click a recommendation to journal it and jump to that passage.

## Tips

- **Use the focus selector** — switch between Christological, Trinitarian, and Theological lenses for different perspectives on the same passage
- **Chain Reference + Intertestament work together** — chains trace a single topic, intertestament traces both testaments' treatment of your passage
- **Bookmark passages** — save studies to history and reload them anytime
- **PDF everything** — every study and workbook has one-click PDF export
- **Privacy first** — struggle entries and prayer requests are API-enforced private
